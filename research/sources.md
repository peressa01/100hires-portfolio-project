## Why these 10?

These aren't people who *write about* LinkedIn strategy in the abstract. Every single one has used LinkedIn as a direct driver of revenue, audience, or company growth. The mix is intentional:

- **Builders in public** (Robinson, Moubeche, Welsh) — show what founder-led content looks like at scale
- **Algorithm & platform experts** (van der Blom) — essential for understanding distribution mechanics
- **Positioning & messaging** (Pierri, Laja, Bourgoin) — the content strategy layer: what to say, not just how often
- **Growth strategy** (Poyar, Gerhardt) — the GTM layer that connects content to pipeline

Together they cover the full stack of what makes LinkedIn content work for B2B SaaS.
