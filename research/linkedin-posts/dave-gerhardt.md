## Post 3 — How Ramp builds an AI-pilled marketing team
**Date:** recent

How Ramp is building an AI-pilled marketing team.

Key lessons from Drew Pinta (growth/data science at Ramp):

- How to measure marketing when the hardest things to measure are often the ones working hardest
- Ramp uses incrementality testing and event studies to put real numbers on brand stunts

## Post 4 — Exit Five CMO Council: community for B2B CMOs
**Date:** recent

I think we're building the best community for B2B VPs / CMOs.

60+ members and growing.

Here's why it's working: CMOs always tell us it's lonely at the top.

When you climb the ranks, you actually have less support. No one on your team understands your world because you're dealing with more strategy vs execution. The other executives in the company aren't marketers. So you feel lonely, unsure of your decisions, out of the loop.

That's why peer communities like the Exit Five CMO Council exist. It's the only space online (and in-person) where you can meet new CMO friends, talk shop, and just vent about career and company-related stuff.
