## Post 3 — 4 LinkedIn Post Lifespan Patterns (from Algorithm Insights Report 2026)
**Date:** recent

Not all LinkedIn posts die the same way. Some explode and vanish. Others build slowly. A few even come back from the dead.

After analysing 1.3M posts, we found 4 distinct lifespan patterns:

**🚀 The Rocket (45% of posts)**
Strong out of the gate. 40% of reach lands within 4 hours, 70% within day one. Burns fast — done by day 5.
- How to nurture: Reply to every comment in the first 4 hours. Ask follow-up questions. Don't wait — the window closes fast.

**📈 The Builder (30% of posts)**
Modest start, steady climb. Engagement builds through day 1 and sustains to day 8. Save rates are disproportionately high.
- How to nurture: Re-enter the thread on days 2-3 with a fresh insight. Let it breathe — don't post over it too quickly.

**🔥 The Afterburner (5% of posts)**
Underperforms days 1-2, then ignites. A share, comment, or save triggers a spike on day 3-4 and sustained distribution for two weeks.
- How to nurture: Double down when it ignites — reply aggressively. Add a comment with additional context or data.
